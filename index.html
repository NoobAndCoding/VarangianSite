Varangian Guard

We are a byzantine division in lionhearts. We are an elite unit of the Byzantine Army serving as personal bodyguards to the Emperor. We are known for primarily composing of recruits from Northern Europe.
As the Emperor's bodyguards we are expected to uphold order as well as maintain discipline within the Empire. As such our members are seen as veteran warriors whose presence can turn the tide of battle.

HR Arxifrouros Arxifrouros are members who have served their time to the Varangian Guard and the Empire and have earned to be a divisional if not factional officer
HR Kapetanios Kapetanios are members who have served their time to the Varangian Guard and the Empire and have earned to be a divisional if not factional officer. They must at least host a training or event once per week. To be into Kapetanios you are approved by the Akolouthos and other Kapetanios
HR Akolouthos ton Varangianon The Akolouthos is basically the leader of the Varangian Guard, responsible of the division and its important decisions and moves.
