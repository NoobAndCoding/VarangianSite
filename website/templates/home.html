Who we are

We, the Varangian Guard are an elite unit of the Byzantine Empire's Army serving as "personal bodyguards" to the emperor. The Varangian Guard was known for being primarily composed of recruits from northern Europe, including mainly Norsemen from Scandinavia but also Anglo-Saxons from England. As the "Emperor's bodyguard" we are expected to uphold order as well as maintain discipline within the Byzantine Empire. As such its members are seen as veteran warriors whose presence can turn the tide of battle.
